**Bestselling Titles & Top-Performing Properties**

**2023 Major Releases:**

-   *Fourth Wing* by Rebecca Yarros (crossover fantasy-romance with sci-fi elements trending upward; massive BookTok success)

-   *The Three-Body Problem* translated English boom (hard sci-fi with philosophical/quasi-mystical elements)

-   *Emily Wilde's Encyclopaedia of Faeries* (paranormal fantasy)

-   *Gideon the Ninth* and *Locked Tomb* series (gothic paranormal + futuristic setting elements)

-   *The Adventures of Amina al-Sirafi* by S.A. Chakraborty (historical fantasy with speculative elements)

-   *Tress of the Emerald Sea* by Brandon Sanderson (standalone adventure, Cosmere science fantasy)

**2024 Standout Performers:**

-   *The Book That Broke the World* by Mark Lawrence (*Library Trilogy*, Book 2; science fantasy + literary fiction; critical acclaim)

-   *Empire of the Damned* by Jay Kristoff (second in trilogy; strong second-book performance; grimdark science fantasy)

-   *Asunder* by Kerstin Hall (paranormal fantasy + philosophical elements)

-   *The Daughters' War* by Christopher Buehlman (epic character-driven grimdark)

-   *Model Home* by Rivers Solomon (horror + speculative elements; multi-generational narrative)

-   *The Strength of the Few* by James Islington (book 2; complex world-building, philosophical depth, described as "Book of the Year" candidate; science fantasy progression)

-   Dune: Part Two film/novel tie-in (continued blockbuster success; demonstrating SF/F film properties drive book sales)

**2025 Major Releases/Anticipated:**

-   *Strength of the Few* by James Islington (continued trajectory; complex progressive magic + intricate plotting)

-   *The Book That Held Her Heart* by Mark Lawrence (*Library Trilogy*, Book 3; conclusion)

-   *Obsidian Awakening* / *Obsidian Revelation* by Sienna Frost (indie grimdark SFF; strong reader enthusiasm)

-   *Hemlock & Silver* by T. Kingfisher (fantasy-horror; strong 2025 critical reception)

-   Star War
