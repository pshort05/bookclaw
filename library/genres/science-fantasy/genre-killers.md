# genre-killers

**The Mentor/Guide**                    Older character teaching protagonist both magical AND technological systems simultaneously                Stable to declining; becoming cliché if not freshly executed

  **Magical Artifact/MacGuffin**          Object with both technological + magical properties driving the plot                                      Stable in space opera, declining in character-driven stories

  **The Great War/Conflict**              Large-scale struggle between technological faction and magical faction, or both against external threat   Stable; less prominent than 2015-2020

  **Hidden Heritage**                     Protagonist discovers they have magical ancestry + technological capability; dual legacy                  Declining; viewers/readers tired of this setup

  **The Prophecy / The Written Future**   Ancient prophecy binding protagonist to specific path; increasingly questioned/subverted                  Declining as straight trope; rising in subverted form

  **Spicy Romance / Sexual Content**      Explicit intimate scenes, often between beings from different magical/technological backgrounds           Rising sharply; particularly in indie/self-published science fantasy
