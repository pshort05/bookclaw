## Dominant Tropes

- Tropes (10 entries) — Recurring character types and plot devices including the Anti-Hero, Hacker/Netrunner, Femme Fatale, Corporate Executive, Fixer, AI, Upgrade Addict, Reluctant Crew, the Heist, and Augmentation Sequences. Each trope entry details how it functions narratively and appears across different punk subgenres.
- Motifs (8 entries) — Recurring sensory and visual elements such as Neon Light, Rain, Chrome/Mirrors, Cyberspace, Urban Decay, Street Level vs. Elite Spaces, Data as Substance, and Addiction & Withdrawal. These create atmosphere and thematic emphasis through repetition and variation.
- Biopunk: Unique themes (body horror, genetic control), tropes (genetically modified protagonists, bio-hackers), and symbols (DNA, mutation, labs)
