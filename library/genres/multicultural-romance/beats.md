## Obligatory Scenes

-  [ ] **POV Clear:** Whose cultural lens are we seeing through? What are their unspoken cultural assumptions in this scene?
-  [ ] **Sensory Details:** Have I included specific, culturally-relevant sights, sounds, smells, tastes, textures?
-  [ ] **Conflict/Goal:** Does this scene advance the romantic plot *through* a cultural dynamic?
-  [ ] **Dialogue Purpose:** Does the dialogue reveal character, expose a cultural difference, or build understanding?
-  [ ] **Emotional Arc:** Does the POV character end the scene with a changed feeling or understanding about their partner's culture or their own?
-  [ ] **Avoids Cliché:** Is this a fresh take, or a stereotype (e.g., "strict parents," "exotic beauty")?
-  [ ] **HEA Alignment:** Does this scene, even if painful, move the couple toward their eventual synthesis?
