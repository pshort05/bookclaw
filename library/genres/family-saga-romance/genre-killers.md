## Failure Modes

- Tone: Nostalgic, immersive, emotionally resonant, and slightly elevated. Avoid overly modern slang (unless in a modern timeline) or clinical detachment.
- 7. Do's and Don'ts
- DON'T: Let the historical research overshadow the emotional relationships.
- DON'T: Resolve the central mystery too early; let it ripple until the end.
- Sins of the Father: Generational trauma. The current generation must resolve the mistakes, debts, or feuds started by their parents.
- Shorthand: Siblings speak in code, inside jokes, and references to childhood events that outsiders (and the love interest) don't understand yet.
- Banned/Avoided: Internet slang (unless by the youngest generation), overly sterile corporate jargon (unless the villain is a developer), disjointed or choppy sentence fragments (prose should flow).
