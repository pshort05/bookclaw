Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] Opening image proves *tone* (ritual, hunt, rot, palace cruelty)
-  [ ] Inciting incident forces a dangerous goal
-  [ ] First strong attraction moment (not necessarily kind)
-  [ ] First power confrontation (rank/magic/leverage)
-  [ ] Lock-in: a bargain/oath/quest that traps them together
-  [ ] Escalation step 1: danger expands (new enemy / law / curse)
-  [ ] Intimacy increases via **forced proximity** or **shared secret**
-  [ ] Midpoint binding event (bond, pact, kiss, blood) + plot reversal
-  [ ] The cost becomes personal (family, body, soul, reputation)
-  [ ] Late Act II betrayal/reveal that breaks trust and worsens the external threat
-  [ ] Dark night: loss/separation/moral failure
-  [ ] Final choice: love requires sacrifice or transformation
-  [ ] External climax resolves the fantasy conflict
-  [ ] Relationship status is explicit (HEA/HFN or clearly signaled tragedy)
- 
-  Dual spine: romance arc + fantasy conflict arc
-  Atmosphere: [gothic / bleak / decadent]
-  Stakes: [war / curse / monster threat / divine punishment]
-  Moral pressure: [betrayal / corruption / sacrifice]
