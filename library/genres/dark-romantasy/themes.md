Core themes and ideas central to this genre.

## Central Themes

-  **Dual spine:** the romance arc and the external fantasy conflict both matter and both resolve.
-  **Atmosphere with teeth:** gloom isn’t decoration; it changes choices (law, curse, hunger, debt, fear).
-  **Moral heat:** love complicates ethics—characters must choose what they’ll sacrifice and what they’ll refuse.
-  **Emotional extremity:** yearning, obsession, fear, devotion, and betrayal are turned up.
