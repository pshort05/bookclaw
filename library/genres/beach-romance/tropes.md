## Dominant Tropes

- Trope Implementation — Detailed guidance for common beach romance tropes (enemies-to-lovers, forced proximity, friends-to-lovers, grumpy/sunshine) with pitfall avoidance
- Naturalistic but refined: Dialogue should sound real without capturing actual speech patterns (real people hesitate, stutter, repeat themselves—characters should not)
- Give each character distinctive voice: Vary vocabulary, rhythm, speech patterns, and emotional expression to differentiate characters

## Rising Tropes

- Avoiding Trope Pitfalls
- Don't substitute trope for character development: Character motivation must exceed trope expectations
- Lean into subversion: Acknowledge trope while bringing fresh angle
- Ensure stakes feel genuine: Readers familiar with tropes need reasons to believe this particular story matters
- Setting research: Coastal locations require specificity—weather patterns, local culture, seasonal shifts, unique geography should be accurate
