Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] **Act I:** Protagonist's need for change is clear. Inciting incident brings them to town. Memorable meet-cute with love interest. Decision to stay is locked in.
-  [ ] **Act II:** Forced proximity scenes build chemistry. Protagonist engages with community (event, meal, helping a local). **Midpoint:** Relationship deepens (first kiss/vulnerable moment). **Complications:** Internal fears & external pressures collide. **Dark Moment:** Major misunderstanding/breakup.
-  [ ] **Act III:** Protagonist reflects, often aided by a secondary character or town symbol. **Climax:** Grand gesture/honest conversation in a meaningful town location. **Resolution:** External plot resolved, place in community secured. **HEA/HFN:** Concrete scene showing couple's future in the town.
