## Essential Checklist

- Use this checklist to outline your Science Fiction Thriller:
- 1. **Concept Hook**: Define the core sci-fi "what if" and the thriller threat it poses.
- 2. **Protagonist Introduction**: Show their ordinary world, skills, and personal stakes.
- 3. **Inciting Incident**: An event that forces the protagonist to engage with the sci-fi threat.
- 4. **Rising Action**: Series of escalating conflicts where the protagonist confronts obstacles, often involving technology.
- 5. **Midpoint Twist**: A revelation that changes the protagonist's understanding or approach.
- 6. **Darkest Moment**: The protagonist faces a major setback, often due to the sci-fi element.
- 7. **Climax**: Final confrontation with the antagonist or threat, using established sci-fi rules.
- 8. **Resolution**: Consequences are shown, and the protagonist's arc is completed.
