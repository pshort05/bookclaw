## Central Themes

- Common themes include:
-  **Ethics of Technology**: Exploring moral implications of advancements like AI, genetic engineering, or surveillance.
-  **Survival and Adaptation**: Characters must adapt to rapidly changing environments or threats.
-  **Trust and Paranoia**: In worlds where reality can be manipulated, trust becomes scarce.
- Incorporate these through character decisions, plot twists, and world-building to add depth and resonance.
-  Read widely in both genres to understand conventions.
-  Watch sci-fi thriller films for pacing and visual inspiration.
-  Revise for consistency in tone and world rules.
- This guide provides a foundation; adapt it to your specific story needs.
