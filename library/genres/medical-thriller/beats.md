## Obligatory Scenes

- For each scene in your medical thriller, ensure:
-  [ ] **Clear Objective:** What does the protagonist want in this scene? (e.g., obtain a sample, convince a colleague).
-  [ ] **Conflict or Tension:** Introduce an obstacle or suspense element (e.g., time pressure, ethical dilemma).
-  [ ] **Medical Authenticity:** Incorporate accurate details without overwhelming the narrative.
-  [ ] **Character Development:** Show growth, relationships, or flaws through actions and dialogue.
-  [ ] **Pacing:** Balance description, dialogue, and action to maintain momentum.
-  [ ] **Hook:** End with a cliffhanger or question to propel readers to the next scene.
