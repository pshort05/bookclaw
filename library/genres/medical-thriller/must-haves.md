## Essential Checklist

- Use this checklist to outline your medical thriller:
-  [ ] **Act 1 Setup:** Introduce protagonist and their medical world; inciting incident occurs (e.g., outbreak discovery).
-  [ ] **Act 1 Turning Point:** Protagonist commits to addressing the conflict, often against opposition.
-  [ ] **Act 2A Rising Action:** Obstacles escalate (e.g., failed tests, political interference); subplots develop.
-  [ ] **Midpoint Shift:** Major revelation or setback changes the game (e.g., true villain exposed).
-  [ ] **Act 2B Complications:** Stakes heighten with personal risks; protagonist faces inner demons.
-  [ ] **Act 3 Climax:** Final confrontation with the threat (e.g., surgical intervention, public health crisis).
-  [ ] **Resolution:** Plot threads resolved; protagonist growth shown; lingering ethical questions addressed.
