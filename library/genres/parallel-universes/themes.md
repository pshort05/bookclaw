*Parallel universe stories explore identity, choice, and whether alternate selves exist as real people deserving moral consideration.*

- **Identity and Selfhood** — what makes you "you"? If alternate versions exist making different choices, do they possess equal validity? Are you your choices or your essence?
- **The Weight of Unchosen Paths** — exploring roads not taken; alternate selves show potential that current-self never pursued; grief for unrealized possibilities
- **Responsibility Across Realities** — if alternate version commits crime, are you responsible? Moral questions about accountability across parallel selves
- **Determinism vs. Choice** — are worlds predetermined or do choices matter? Can you change your universe or are consequences inevitable regardless of effort?
- **Connection Beyond Self** — relationships with alternate versions of loved ones; exploring whether love transcends circumstance or is circumstantial
- **Power and Privilege Across Worlds** — how do systemic inequities play out differently across universes? Which margins receive privilege in which worlds?
- **The Cost of Knowledge** — knowing your alternate path changes your relationship to current life; awareness becomes burden
- **Nostalgia and Longing** — yearning for unrealized versions of self or loved ones; exploring whether perfect version exists in alternate universe
