# Women's Sports Romance — Themes

- **Female athletic excellence as non-negotiable** — the heroine's competitive drive, physical strength, and ambition are never sacrificed or diminished for romantic connection.
- **Respect & mutual admiration** — the love interest champions the heroine's athletic identity and celebrates her excellence.
- **Balancing ambition with connection** — exploring how high-level competition and romantic partnership can coexist and strengthen each other.
- **Female solidarity & community** — celebrating friendships and alliances among female athletes; rejecting competitive cattiness.
- **Overcoming systemic barriers** — navigating unequal pay, media bias, sponsorship gaps, and institutional sexism in women's athletics.
- **Physical empowerment & bodily autonomy** — celebrating a strong, capable female body and sexual agency.
