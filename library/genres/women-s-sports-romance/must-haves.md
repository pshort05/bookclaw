# Women's Sports Romance — Must-Haves

- [ ] **Establish the heroine's athletic competence early.** Readers must see her training, competing, and excelling at her sport within the first few chapters; she is not a "sporty girl," she is a legitimate athlete.
- [ ] **Ground the sport specifically.** Include authentic details about training, competition structure, team dynamics, and physical/mental demands of that discipline; "green-screen" sports feel hollow.
- [ ] **Maintain the heroine's athletic ambition throughout.** Her romantic relationship never requires her to compromise or abandon her competitive goals.
- [ ] **Create high-stakes external conflict rooted in competition.** The external obstacle is grounded in athletic stakes (a rival, a championship, an injury, a career-defining moment), not arbitrary obstacles.
- [ ] **Honor female athleticism as sexy.** The heroine's strength, competence, and physical power are attractive; celebrate rather than minimize this.
- [ ] **Deliver mutual respect between the leads.** Both romantic partners respect each other's ambition, competence, and athletic identity.
