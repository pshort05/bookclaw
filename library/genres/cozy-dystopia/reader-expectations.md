## Setting

Cozy Dystopia explores the quiet persistence of humanity within an oppressive or broken world. The focus is not on rebellion, but on the small, meaningful acts of creating comfort, community, and beauty despite the grim backdrop. The central theme is finding warmth in a cold world. Think of a secret garden in a concrete megacity, or a warm teashop in a surveillance state.
