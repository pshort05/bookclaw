Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] **Act I: The Haunted Return**
-  [ ] Protagonist arrives/returns to a decaying Southern locale.
-  [ ] Atmosphere of heat, decay, and quiet dread is established.
-  [ ] Meets locals who speak in riddles or warnings.
-  [ ] Glimpses the initial "rot"—a strange event, a grotesque figure, a disturbing heirloom.
-  [ ] **Act II: Unveiling the Rot**
-  [ ] Protagonist investigates, digging into family/town history.
-  [ ] Encounters blatantly grotesque characters or phenomena.
-  [ ] Learns of a past sin (slavery, murder, betrayal, pact).
-  [ ] The supernatural force (or crushing madness) becomes personal, targeting the protagonist.
-  [ ] **Act III: Confrontation with the Past**
-  [ ] The full truth of the historical sin is revealed, often tying to the protagonist's bloodline.
-  [ ] Final confrontation in a symbolic location (swamp, mansion, church).
-  [ ] Protagonist must choose: destroy, join, flee, or become the new keeper of the cycle.
-  [ ] **Resolution: The Lingering Echo**
-  [ ] Show the aftermath. Is the place purged, or does the quiet dread return?
-  [ ] Hint that the story, or the sin, is not fully over.
