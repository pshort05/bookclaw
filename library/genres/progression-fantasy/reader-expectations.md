Readers expect a narrative that delivers on specific emotional and structural promises.

## Tone

Readers expect focused, character-driven narratives with rising technical mastery. The tone is often introspective—protagonist perceiving incremental growth—combined with escalating challenge and danger.

## Pacing

The pacing should build steadily through skill acquisition, cultivation milestones, and power tests. Early chapters establish foundation mechanics; mid-story accelerates through advancement and enemy encounters; late chapters converge multiple power systems.

## Setting

The setting provides a framework for progression mechanics—whether a cultivation realm, game system, or academy. The world itself reinforces incremental power growth central to the genre.

## Archetypes

The protagonist is often a young talent or underdog discovering unexpected potential. Cultivators, dungeon delvers, system-bound heroes, and academy students are classic archetypes.

## Length & Format

Standard novel length for this genre is 70,000–120,000 words. Series are common, with each book marking a major advancement.
