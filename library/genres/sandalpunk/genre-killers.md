# genre-killers

#### Tropes Declining

##### The Roman Republic/Empire Re-skin

A lazy setting that is essentially ancient Rome with steam added, complete with lazy Latin-esque names, toga-clad senators, and gladiatorial games, but lacking in original world-building or engagement with the actual diversity of the classical world. Readers now demand more authenticity and creativity.

##### The Wise Old Greek Mentor

The obligatory elderly, bearded philosopher who spouts cryptic wisdom. This archetype is seen as cliché, especially if he exists solely to deliver exposition and then die. Modern works favor mentors who are flawed, active, or from non-Greek traditions.

##### The Lost Hyper-Advanced Precursor Race

The explanation for all advanced technology being 'left behind by the Atlanteans' or a similar lost civilization. This undercuts the core Sandalpunk premise of human ingenuity and feels like a cheap narrative shortcut. Contemporary works prefer technologies developed by the cultures in the story.
