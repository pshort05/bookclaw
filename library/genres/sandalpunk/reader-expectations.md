## Setting

Sandalpunk is a subgenre of speculative fiction that reimagines antiquity—typically Classical Greece, Rome, Egypt, Persia, or other pre-industrial Mediterranean/Near Eastern cultures—through a lens of advanced (often anachronistic) technology, social progressivism, and a punk ethos. It critiques power structures while celebrating ingenuity and marginalized voices from historical settings.
