## Obligatory Scenes

-  [ ] **Environmental Pressure:** What specific climate stressor is active in this scene (heat, rain, bad air, scarcity)? How does it physically affect the characters?
-  [ ] **Resource Context:** What resources (water, power, breathable air, data-bandwidth) are scarce? How does this influence character decisions and dialogue?
-  [ ] **Clue Integration:** If this is an investigative scene, is the clue or information gathered dependent on or revealed by an environmental factor?
-  [ ] **Sensory Detail:** Have you used at least one specific, non-visual sense (sound, smell, texture, taste) to ground the scene in this world?
-  [ ] **Dialogue Subtext:** Are characters negotiating for more than just information? What unspoken resource or need underlies their words?
-  [ ] **Plot Obstacle:** Does the environment itself create a minor obstacle or cost to the action in this scene (e.g., taking time to don protective gear, a solar flare disrupting comms)?
-  [ ] **Thematic Echo:** Does a detail in the scene (a broken object, a character's habit) reflect the larger themes of adaptation, decay, or resilience?
