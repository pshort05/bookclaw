Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] **Inciting Incident:** Is the crime uniquely caused/enabled by a climate-impacted world?
-  [ ] **Investigator's Motive:** Are the protagonist's stakes personal and tied to the climate setting?
-  [ ] **First Clue:** Is the initial evidence intertwined with an environmental detail?
-  [ ] **Suspect Field:** Do all suspects have credible, climate-related motives (greed, survival, ideology, protection)?
-  [ ] **Environmental Setbacks:** Do at least two major investigation setbacks arise from weather or resource failures?
-  [ ] **Midpoint Twist:** Does the revelation connect the crime to a larger systemic conspiracy or truth?
-  [ ] **Weaponized Environment:** Is the environment or climate tech used as a direct threat against the protagonist?
-  [ ] **Climax Setting:** Does the final confrontation occur during or because of a significant environmental event?
-  [ ] **Systemic Revelation:** Does solving the mystery expose a broader failing (corporate, governmental, social)?
-  [ ] **Denouement:** Does the ending show adaptation/change, not a return to a pre-crisis "normal"?
