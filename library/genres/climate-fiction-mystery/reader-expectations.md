## Setting

Climate Fiction Mystery is a hybrid genre that merges the investigative structure and revelatory drive of mystery fiction with the setting, stakes, and ethical framework of climate fiction (cli-fi). The central plot involves solving a crime, puzzle, or conspiracy that is intrinsically linked to a climate-disrupted world. The "mystery" is often not just "whodunit," but "what was done," "who covered it up," and what the systemic cost to both humanity and the planet has been.
