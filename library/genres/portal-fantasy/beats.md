*Portal fantasy typically follows a discovery-arc, taking readers through the protagonist's learning journey.*

1. **Mundane World Establishment** — introduce protagonist in ordinary settings; establish what's missing or unfulfilled in their current life
2. **Portal Encounter** — magical gateway appears or is discovered; protagonist crosses threshold (willingly or accidentally) into fantasy world
3. **Initial Disorientation** — fantasy world's strangeness overwhelms; protagonist experiences culture shock, magic shock, resource disorientation
4. **Mentor/Guide Encounters** — protagonist meets character(s) explaining fantasy-world basics, teaching magic system, or offering alliance/protection
5. **World Exploration and Learning** — protagonist gradually understands geography, politics, magic system, social structures; finds role or purpose in fantasy setting
6. **Conflict Escalates** — larger threat emerges (political crisis, magical danger, prophecy stakes); protagonist becomes entangled in consequential events
7. **Protagonist's Choice Point** — protagonist must decide between loyalties, courses of action, or the fundamental question of returning versus remaining
8. **Resolution and Transformation** — conflict resolves; protagonist's decision about world-choice clarified; ending shows transformed understanding and altered future
