# Urban Fantasy Romance Reader Expectations

What readers expect and demand from this genre.

- Gritty, atmospheric urban settings with supernatural hidden layer beneath contemporary city
- Compelling romantic relationship development with real emotional stakes and HEA
- Significant external plot and conflict (40-50% minimum page time); romance enhances rather than overshadows primary story
- Competent, scarred protagonists with distinct personalities; witty banter and snark
- High-stakes genuine danger with believable consequences to actions and magic use
- Magic systems with clear rules, costs, and limitations; magic should complicate rather than solve problems
- Slow-burn romance establishing emotional and intellectual compatibility before physical intimacy
- Found family and belonging themes; characters are often outcasts choosing their pack
- Moral complexity and character agency; grey area decisions and difficult choices
- Sensory details grounding fantasy in concrete urban reality; specific landmarks and settings
- Dual POV showing both romantic leads' internal experiences and distinct voices
- Happily Ever After (HEA) in romantic pairing; relationship resolves satisfactorily
