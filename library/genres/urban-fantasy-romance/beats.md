# Urban Fantasy Romance Story Beats

Obligatory scenes and structural progression.

- Ordinary world: Protagonist established in gritty urban setting; hints at hidden supernatural layer
- The Masquerade revealed: Protagonist encounters evidence of hidden magical world (creature, spell, unexplained event)
- Meet-cute with love interest: First encounter with romantic lead; antagonism, attraction, or forced interaction
- Growing awareness: Protagonist drawn deeper into supernatural world; emotional and intellectual connection develops
- Obstacles established: External threats, faction conflicts, or forbidden relationship revealed; stakes clarified
- Midpoint escalation: Danger intensifies; relationship reaches critical moment (first kiss, emotional vulnerability, forced choice)
- Dark moment: Separation, betrayal, or choice between love and duty; relationship and external plot converge
- Climactic confrontation: Final battle or confrontation with external antagonist; relationship tested under ultimate pressure
- Resolution and sacrifice: Love interest's choice/sacrifice proves commitment; world-saving action and personal commitment align
- HEA: Romantic relationship secured; internal and external conflicts resolved; protagonist's place in supernatural world established
