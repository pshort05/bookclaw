# Urban Fantasy Romance Must-Haves

Essential elements that define the genre.

- Contemporary urban setting (recognizable modern city) with hidden supernatural layer seamlessly integrated
- Rule-based magic system with clear costs, limitations, and consequences; magic should not be infinite or convenient
- Romantic relationship with Happily Ever After (HEA) resolution; both protagonists commit to each other
- Significant external plot and conflict (40-50% minimum page time); romance enhances rather than overshadows primary story
- Character-specific distinct voices, vocabulary, and speech patterns that differ between POV characters
- Sensory details grounding fantasy in concrete urban reality; specific landmarks, infrastructure, economics integrated with magic
- High-stakes consequences that feel real; danger is genuine; actions have believable repercussions
- Personal, emotionally resonant stakes; character motivation rooted in internal needs not abstract threat
- Built-in tension from conflict and obstacles; relationship does not come easy; protagonists must overcome real barriers
- Competent protagonists with believable flaws; heroes and heroines are skilled but vulnerable, scarred but not broken
- Integration of magic with modern infrastructure, economics, society; show how supernatural beings organize and interact with mundane world
- Romance as natural part of story stakes; love complicates and enhances external conflict; partners are on different sides of key tensions
