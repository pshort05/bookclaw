# Urban Fantasy Romance Tropes

Common patterns and character dynamics readers expect.

- Enemies-to-Lovers: Natural antagonism between couple (hunter and hunted, cop and criminal, opposing factions)
- Fated Mates or The Mating Bond: Biological, magical, or destinal force irrevocably linking protagonists; often acknowledged by beast before man
- Slow-Burn Romance: Attraction established early but resisted; anticipation built through near-touches, charged dialogue, denied consummation
- Forced Proximity: Stuck together in safe house, bound by spell or investigation, trapped in confined space
- The Masquerade (The Veil): Hidden magical world beneath mundane reality; plot often hinges on threat to expose secret
- The Power Imbalance: One character significantly stronger/older/more powerful, but other holds emotional leverage or critical skill
- Found Family: Pack, coven, or chosen family more important than blood relations; often involves rebellion against corrupt authority
- The Reveal: Moment when human love interest sees true supernatural form (monster, beast, fae) and accepts rather than rejects
- The "Touch Her and Die" Alpha: Leader of faction (Pack Alpha, Vampire Prince) becomes irrationally protective and possessive
- Magic Awakening or The Turned: Protagonist begins as human (or thinks she is) but discovers latent power or is turned into creature
- Forbidden Love: Relationship violates law, faction rules, or cosmic alignment; carries real danger
- The Neutral Ground (Sanctuary): Location where violence is magically forbidden, allowing different species to interact
