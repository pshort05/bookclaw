*These are non-negotiable; missing any creates a work that may be romantic or funny, but not both in equilibrium.*

- **Authentic Humor** — genuinely funny moments, witty dialogue, comedic situations; not just clever quips or forced jokes
- **Balanced Tone** — comedy never completely undermines romance's emotional stakes; feelings matter while humor entertains
- **Genuine Chemistry** — protagonist attraction must feel earned through banter, shared values, complementary personalities, or genuine spark
- **Flawed, Relatable Protagonists** — ordinary people navigating authentic modern challenges; perfection kills reader investment and humor
- **Witty Dialogue and Banter** — sharp, rapid-fire exchanges revealing character, advancing attraction, generating humor simultaneously
- **Earned Happy Ending** — resolution through character growth and meaningful obstacle-overcoming; not mere circumstance removal or external rescue
- **Authentic Contemporary Setting** — modern challenges (dating apps, social media, hybrid work, financial stress) that shape romantic possibility
- **Character Development Tied to Romance** — protagonists grow through love; romantic relationships catalyze meaningful personal transformation
