*Avoid these patterns—they undermine authenticity romance western demands.*

- ✗ **Frontier as Backdrop** — western setting is cosmetic; identical contemporary romance with horses replacing cars
- ✗ **Instant Frontier Expertise** — protagonist from civilization becomes expert rancher/frontiersperson unrealistically quickly
- ✗ **Ignored Frontier Hardship** — romance ignores genuine deprivation, danger, isolation that characterizes frontier life
- ✗ **Rescue Romance** — one protagonist constantly rescuing other; undermines partnership frontier survival requires
- ✗ **Whitewashed Frontier** — erasing Indigenous peoples, Black settlers, or other historical populations from frontier world-building
- ✗ **Male Pioneer, Female Passive** — woman arriving passively while man shows her frontier; undermines female agency and competence
- ✗ **Modern Attitudes/Language** — contemporary sensibilities/speech patterns breaking frontier authenticity
- ✗ **Convenient Resolution** — romance obstacles magically resolve; frontier hardships disappear enabling easy happiness
