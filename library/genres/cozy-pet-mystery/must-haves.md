Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] Introduce sleuth and pet within the first chapter.
-  [ ] Establish the cozy setting and community dynamics early.
-  [ ] Inciting incident: a non-violent crime occurs by chapter 2 or 3.
-  [ ] Investigation scenes include pet involvement and clue gathering.
-  [ ] Red herrings are introduced and resolved logically.
-  [ ] Climax reveals the culprit through deduction, not confrontation.
-  [ ] Resolution shows community harmony restored and loose ends tied.
-  [ ] Pet's contribution is acknowledged and celebrated.
