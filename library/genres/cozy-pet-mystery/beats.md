## Obligatory Scenes

-  [ ] Set the scene with cozy details: time of day, weather, interior comforts.
-  [ ] Introduce or advance a mystery element through action or dialogue.
-  [ ] Include pet interaction that adds value—clue, humor, or character insight.
-  [ ] Maintain a light tone; avoid suspense that borders on thriller.
-  [ ] End with a hook or transition that propels the story forward.
-  [ ] Ensure scene aligns with overall story structure and pacing.
