## Obligatory Scenes

- Before finishing a scene, ask:
- 1.  **Where are the shadows?** (Literal lighting or figurative secrets).
- 2.  **Is the dialogue subtextual?** (Are they talking about tea, or are they talking about betrayal?)
- 3.  **Is there a sensory anchor?** (The smell of rain, the taste of stale donuts, the sound of a ticking clock).
- 4.  **Did we advance the mystery?** (New clue or new suspicion).
