Tropes are the recurring patterns that readers expect and enjoy in this genre.

## Dominant Tropes

-    **Atmospheric Immersion:** Focus on weather (rain, fog, wind), lighting (shadows, neon signs in shop windows, flickering streetlamps), and sensory details (the smell of old books and ozone).
-    **The Cynical Voice:** The narration (POV) should lean sardonic, witty, and slightly world-weary, even if the protagonist is a librarian.
-    *Example:* "The sun came up like a guilty verdict, bright and uninvited, exposing the cracks in the cobblestones of Maple Street."
-    **Punchy Descriptions:** Use short, impactful sentences for action and observation. Use metaphors related to shadows, ghosts, gambles, or broken things.
-    **Subjective Interiority:** Spend time in the sleuth’s head analyzing the psychology of the suspects. The "darkness" in this genre is psychological, not physical.
