Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] **The Mood Setter:** Is it raining? Is it night? Is the protagonist brooding?
-  [ ] **The "Clean" Body:** Found in an ironic location?
-  [ ] **The "Hook":** Why can't the sleuth walk away? (Personal stake).
-  [ ] **The First Interrogation:** Sleuth meets the "Femme/Homme Fatale" figure.
-  [ ] **The Underbelly Reveal:** The cute town has a secret ring (gambling, forgery, affair).
-  [ ] **The Low Point:** Sleuth is threatened (non-violently) or warned off; doubts themselves.
-  [ ] **The Reveal:** A parlor scene or a one-on-one confrontation.
-  [ ] **The Moral:** The crime is solved, but the protagonist remains wary.
