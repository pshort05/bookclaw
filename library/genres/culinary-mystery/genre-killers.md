Avoid these mistakes that can undermine your story.

## Failure Modes

-  **Pitfall**: Food scenes stall plot
- Fix**: Tie preparation to clue discovery (e.g., chopping herbs reveals hidden object)
-  **Pitfall**: Stereotyped "foodie" characters
- Fix**: Give chefs/suspects distinct culinary philosophies (molecular gastronomy vs. farm-to-table)
-  **Pitfall**: Unrealistic investigations
- Fix**: Limit sleuth's access to police info; use food credentials for access (e.g., "As the caterer, I need allergy details")
