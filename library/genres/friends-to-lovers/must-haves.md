Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] **Act 1:** Established friendship shown in action.
-  [ ] **Inciting Incident:** "The Glitch" – a moment of changed perception.
-  [ ] **Initial Denial/Rationalization.**
-  [ ] **Act 2:** Escalating tension via near-misses, jealousy, increased proximity.
-  [ ] **Catalyzing Event** forces a reevaluation (e.g., one gets a date).
-  [ ] **Awkward Phase / Friendship Strain.**
-  [ ] **Dark Moment:** The friendship ruptures or seems lost.
-  [ ] **Act 3:** Realization of feelings (often during separation).
-  [ ] **Grand Gesture/Confession** that names the fear and the hope.
-  [ ] **First Real Kiss / Commitment.**
-  [ ] **Resolution:** Navigating the new, evolved relationship.
