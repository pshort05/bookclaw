## Rising Tropes

- The Body in the Trunk: The defining trope of the genre. The problem isn't just the murder; it's the logistics of disposal. The body is heavy, the trunk won't close, and a cop just pulled you over for a broken taillight.
- 3. Motifs (Recurring Structures)
- Key Trope: The Wood Chipper. Violence is graphic, messy, and loud. It is not stylized; it is awkward and gross.
- Key Trope: The Amateur Sleuth. A baker or librarian who knows more than the police.
- Key Trope: The Gadget Fail. The pen-bomb leaks ink; the laser watch burns the wearer's wrist.
