## Essential Checklist

- Temporal: Character's timeline is non-linear; reader must reconstruct order
- Thriller: Wireheading offered as temptation; protagonist must resist
- Thriller: Love as leverage; protagonist must choose between loved one and escape
- Thriller: Protagonist pinned down; must escape by will/hacking alone
- Techno-thriller: Precise countdown; hacker must race clock
- Function: What protagonist must learn to break; what separates possible from impossible.

- Reality is Suspect. The protagonist is not just fighting an antagonist; they are fighting the setting itself. Every detail of the world is potentially artificial. Paranoia is not a flaw—it is a survival mechanic. The narrative must constantly tread the line between "mental breakdown" and "terrible truth."
- No Magic w/o Mechanics: Do not treat reality-bending as magic. It is hacking, exploits, or admin privileges. It must have a cost or a rule set.
