- Common Tropes
- Trope: The Magical Mistake. A spell goes wrong in the kitchen, and a customer turns into a newt or falls in love with a lamp, creating a subplot.
- Trope: The Family Recipe Book. An ancient grimoire that has been passed down, containing both recipes and secrets.
- Trope: The Cursed Tome. The weapon is a book that kills anyone who reads page 13.

## Rising Tropes

- Trope: The Labyrinthine Library. The stacks are endless, shift positions, and contain ghosts of past librarians.
- Trope: The Skeptic. The protagonist tries to explain magic away using science, often hilariously.
- Trope: Technology vs. Magic. The protagonist uses a smartphone or flashlight, which the locals view as a powerful artifact.
- Trope: The Old War Buddy. A scary orc or demon from the protagonist's past shows up, not to fight, but to have a latte and provide a clue.
- Trope: Swords to Plowshares. The protagonist uses their legendary weapon for mundane tasks (e.g., using a Vorpal Sword to slice cheese).
