## Obligatory Scenes

-  [ ] **Historical Anchor:** Have I included 1-2 specific, period-accurate sensory details?
-  [ ] **Character Goal:** What does the POV character want in this scene, informed by their historical context?
-  [ ] **Period Obstacle:** What historical reality (class, gender, law, technology, custom) makes achieving that goal difficult?
-  [ ] **Social Dynamics:** Do the greetings, forms of address, and body language reflect the period's social hierarchy?
-  [ ] **Subtext:** Is there a gap between what is said and what is meant, appropriate to the era's social constraints?
-  [ ] **Revelation or Change:** Does the protagonist learn something new (about the world, a person, or themselves) that changes their understanding?
-  [ ] **Consequence:** Does the scene's outcome push the plot forward by creating a new historical or personal complication?
