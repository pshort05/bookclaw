- 7. Forbidden Tropes (The "Don'ts")
- No Stupid Misunderstandings: Conflict should not rely on a secret that could be solved by one conversation. If they don't talk, there must be a deep psychological reason why.
- Technique: Maintain clear chapter identification of POV character; avoid shifting POV within chapters (head-hopping)
- Integration Principle: Sexual scenes should advance emotional intimacy or character development; avoid purely mechanical descriptions
- Love Triangle: Reader frustration with divided affection; avoid unless serving specific narrative function beyond emotional uncertainty
- Passive Female Protagonists: Avoid heroines requiring rescue or masculine fixing of problems
- Mental Health Integration: Authentically portray anxiety, depression, PTSD, and past relational trauma as ongoing character elements (avoid romanticization of mental illness)
- Emotional Escalation: Ensure consistent emotional intensity increase across narrative; avoid prolonged plateau or regression
- Denouement Appropriateness: Provide sufficient resolution of both emotional and circumstantial obstacles; avoid abrupt or underdeveloped endings
- Worldbuilding Balance: Avoid excessive paranormal system exposition that overshadows romantic relationship development
