# Sports Romance — Tropes

Pick three to six that serve your central question. Tropes are flavor, not obligations;
intertwine them with the sport's mechanics so the athletic context drives the trope.

- **Rivals to Lovers** — same sport, competing teams, or competing for the same roster spot.
- **Enemies to Lovers** — fierce initial animosity masking undeniable chemistry.
- **Forced Proximity** — shared training rinks, road-trip hotel rooms, or PR tours.
- **Fake Dating** — to repair a tarnished PR image or secure a brand sponsorship.
- **Grumpy / Sunshine** — the brooding, low-energy athlete and the hyper-optimistic love interest.
- **Coach/Player or Manager/Player** — high-stakes forbidden romance with career-ending potential.
- **Brother's Best Friend / Teammate's Sister** — breaking the ultimate locker-room rule.
- **Injury / Comeback Story** — vulnerability forced by physical trauma and the fight to return.
- **Player Tamed by Love** — the notorious playboy who risks his reputation for one person.
- **Marriage of Convenience** — contractual arrangements to secure visas, draft eligibility, or inheritance.
