# Sports Romance — Themes

Choose two or three to form your thematic spine. Every scene should test these ideas.

- **The body as identity and commodity** — the physical realities of elite sport: injuries, performance anxiety, and the terrifyingly short shelf-life of an athletic career.
- **Vulnerability vs. performance** — peeling back the public, media-trained persona to reveal the fragile, authentic self underneath.
- **Found family and team loyalty** — how the locker room functions as a surrogate family, and the friction when romantic love threatens team cohesion.
- **The ethical weight of fame** — balancing personal desire with public scrutiny, sponsorships, and fan expectations.
