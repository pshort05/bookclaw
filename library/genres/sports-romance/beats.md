# Sports Romance Story Beats

Obligatory scenes and structural beats.

- Ordinary world: athlete defined by sport
- Meet-cute linked to sport
- Clear rule/reason preventing relationship
- Midpoint: major game with relationship turn
- Complications from sports world
- Dark moment: choice between sport and love
- Character realizations during separation
- Grand gesture tied to sports world
- Final test resolving both threads
- HEA showing union
