# Sports Romance — Beats

Use the *Romancing the Beat* structure (Setup, Falling in Love, Retreating, Fighting for
Love) strictly woven into the **Athletic Pressure System**. The structural signature of the
genre: the romantic arc and the sports-season timeline must peak at the *exact same point*.

## Obligatory Scenes (readers would feel cheated without these)
- **The collision / meet cute** — occurs in a high-pressure athletic context (a post-game presser, an injury evaluation, a rink scheduling mix-up).
- **The forced adhesion** — a structural sport reason (trade, PR mandate, assigned tutoring) locks them together.
- **The locker-room / away-game vulnerability** — the mid-point shift where external pressures of the sport force emotional walls to drop, usually far from home.
- **The athletic dark night (the third-act breakup)** — a career-threatening event (a devastating injury, a trade deadline, a leaked PR scandal) forces a choice between the sport and the romance.
- **The grand gesture at the big game** — the emotional climax happening simultaneously with the competitive climax (championship, draft day, finals). The apology must be earned.
- **The off-season epilogue** — proof of the HEA, showing the characters whole-hearted and surviving the pressures of the sport.
