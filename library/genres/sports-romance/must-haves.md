# Sports Romance — Must-Haves (non-negotiable checklist)

Skip these and it isn't a sports romance. Treat as a revision checklist.

- [ ] **Athletic stakes drive romance** — every major romantic scene is complicated, delayed, or amplified by a sport-specific context (training schedules, contracts, public image).
- [ ] **Dual climax** — the romance's emotional climax aligns perfectly with the climax of the athletic season.
- [ ] **Physical reality on the page** — the athlete's body is not window dressing; exhaustion, physical conditioning, and injury influence character decisions.
- [ ] **On-page team dynamics** — banter, locker-room politics, and the judgment of teammates or coaches exist to raise the stakes.
- [ ] **Explicit consent** — even in dark or aggressive sports romance, on-page verbal or implied consent is non-negotiable.
- [ ] **A heavy grovel** — if a character deeply betrays the other, they deliver a grand, usually public gesture. Easy forgiveness is unacceptable.
