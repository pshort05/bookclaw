# Sports Romance — Comparable Titles

Use these to calibrate tone, structure, and reader expectations.

- **Icebreaker by Hannah Grace** — the gold standard for college forced-proximity; balances the discipline of figure skating with the chaos of hockey team dynamics.
- **The Deal by Elle Kennedy** — a masterclass in witty banter and the fake-dating trope, organically blending humor with emotional depth in a college setting.
- **Heated Rivalry by Rachel Reid** — demonstrates how intense, on-ice athletic rivalry actively fuels and mirrors the off-ice, steamy enemies-to-lovers tension.
- **The Wall of Winnipeg and Me by Mariana Zapata** — the blueprint for the sports-romance slow burn and marriage of convenience; excels at the quiet, grueling realities of an NFL star's life.
- **Throttled by Lauren Asher** — high-speed, high-ego Formula One racing where global public-identity pressures and teammate/rival dynamics make every interaction dangerously charged.
