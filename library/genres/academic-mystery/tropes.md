## Rising Tropes

- Recurring structures, contrasts, and literary devices.
- Trope Variation: The Unreliable Narrator. The protagonist is usually part of the guilty group, retelling the story with bias and nostalgia.
- Trope Variation: The Amateur Sleuth Professor. A literature or history professor uses their academic niche knowledge to solve the crime.
- Trope Variation: The Rumor Mill. Information spreads via gossip and text chains rather than formal investigation.
- Trope Variation: The Accident. The death looks like a lab accident (chemical spill, virus leak), but was engineered.
