Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] **Inciting Incident:** Forces protagonist into a no-win situation or a conflict with a corrupt system.
-  [ ] **Rising Action:** Protagonist's attempts to gain control lead to escalating costs and moral compromises.
-  [ ] **Midpoint Reversal:** A revelation shows the situation is worse than imagined, or an ally betrays them.
-  [ ] **Descent:** Protagonist embraces harder methods, loses hope, or is physically/mentally broken.
-  [ ] **Climax:** A final, brutal confrontation where victory is ambiguous or achieved through terrible means.
-  [ ] **Resolution:** The immediate threat may be gone, but the world remains grim, the protagonist is scarred, and new problems emerge.
