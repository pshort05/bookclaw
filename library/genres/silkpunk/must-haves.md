Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] **Act I:** Established a world of beautiful, intricate tech/society with a visible flaw or tension?
-  [ ] **Act I:** Inciting incident disrupts harmony through theft, demand, discovery, or failure?
-  [ ] **Act I:** Protagonist's commitment is tied to craft, duty, knowledge, or philosophical pursuit?
-  [ ] **Act II:** Protagonist deepens specialized knowledge (seeks master, experiments, deciphers)?
-  [ ] **Act II:** Opposition is intellectual/artistic (flawed designs, guild sanctions, ethical debates) as well as physical?
-  [ ] **Act II:** Midpoint reveals a key fusion of old principle and new insight?
-  [ ] **Act II:** Allies represent collective ingenuity; dilemmas involve ethics of innovation?
-  [ ] **Act III:** Climax is a duel of crafts, demonstration, or transformative act, not just a battle?
-  [ ] **Act III:** Resolution restores balance or creates a new synthesis through knowledge/artistry?
-  [ ] **Act III:** Ending shows changed world and protagonist's growth in understanding their craft's role?
