Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

- Act I (25%)**
-  [ ] Crime discovered, disrupting a social facade.
-  [ ] Protagonist introduced with clear personal wound.
-  [ ] First clue suggests hidden depth.
-  [ ] Stakes established (professional & personal).
- Act II (55%)**
-  [ ] Investigation proceeds with procedural realism.
-  [ ] Protagonist's personal life unravels in parallel.
-  [ ] Suspects reveal societal hypocrisies.
-  [ ] Midpoint reversal raises stakes/concept.
-  [ ] False leads and dead ends create frustration.
- Act III (20%)**
-  [ ] Personal and professional crises force moral choice.
-  [ ] Confrontation is psychological, not physical.
-  [ ] Case resolved, but at a personal cost.
-  [ ] Final image emphasizes melancholy and lingering unease.
