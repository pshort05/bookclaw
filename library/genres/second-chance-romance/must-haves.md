-   **Forced proximity frameworks** -- Weddings, holidays, family gatherings, shared road trips, or professional obligations trap exes together long enough to work through issues.[^[\[5\]]^](#fn5)[^[\[30\]]^](#fn30)[^[\[4\]]^](#fn4)

-   **Trauma‑linked separation** -- Breakups triggered by bereavement, mental health crises, or violence, with reunions requiring the couple to address trauma directly rather than treating it as a mere misunderstanding.[^[\[15\]]^](#fn15)[^[\[19\]]^](#fn19)

Online trope lists and reader discussions consistently place **second chance romance** alongside enemies‑to‑lovers, friends‑to‑lovers, and forced proximity as one of the major story-based romance tropes, reflecting its status as a versatile narrative template rather than a narrow niche.[^[\[2\]]^](#fn2)[^[\[27\]]^](#fn27)
