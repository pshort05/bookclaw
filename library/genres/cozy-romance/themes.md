# Cozy Romance — Themes

The gentle ideas a cozy romance explores. Choose two or three; keep them affirming, since
the whole point is comfort.

- **Home and belonging** — finding the place and people where you fit; community as a warm net.
- **Healthy love looks like communication** — the relationship models talking, listening, and choosing each other on purpose; this is the genre's quiet thesis.
- **Healing without trauma-mining** — characters grow past a small fear or old hurt gently, without the book wallowing in pain.
- **The comfort of small things** — a good meal, a familiar shop, a shared ritual; ordinary life made tender.
- **Second chances and fresh starts** — a new town, a new season, permission to begin again.
- **Chosen family and community care** — neighbors and friends who show up; love widening a life.
- **Slowing down** — contentment over ambition; a deliberate antidote to a high-stress world.
