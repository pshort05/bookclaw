*These elements separate private eye narratives from mysteries; without them, you lose the investigation authenticity PIs demand.*

- **Authentic Investigation Methodology** — PI uses realistic research, surveillance, interview, and database techniques; avoid magical evidence appearance
- **Moral and Ethical Complexity** — PI operates in gray areas; bent rules, questionable methods, compromised outcomes explored honestly
- **Client Relationship Authenticity** — PI-client dynamic includes power imbalance, trust issues, potential manipulation; relationships aren't simple
- **Hardboiled Tone with Genuine Care** — cynicism as protective stance masking real investment in client/case outcomes; emotional depth beneath tough exterior
- **Jurisdictional Realism** — PI operates outside official law enforcement; understands police, legal limitations, private investigator legal constraints
- **Systemic Injustice Acknowledgment** — investigation reveals institutional failures; recognition that official system doesn't protect vulnerable people
- **Personal Cost** — investigation affects PI's relationships, health, perspective; accumulating trauma and cynicism visible in character development
- **Imperfect Resolution** — truth revealed but clean justice elusive; some cases can't be solved; some truths cause more pain than ignorance
