*Private eye narratives typically follow investigation arcs from initial hire through revelation and resolution.*

1. **Case Acquisition** — desperate client hires PI; establish case parameters, client motivation, and initial investigation direction
2. **Initial Investigation** — PI pursues obvious leads, interviews relevant parties, gathers basic case information
3. **Investigation Deepens** — PI discovers contradictions, false leads, or evidence suggesting deeper complications than initially apparent
4. **Dangerous Knowledge Emerges** — investigation reveals information powerful people want hidden; threats or pressure applied to PI
5. **Client Secret Revealed** — PI discovers client lied about case purpose or investigation leads into unexpected territory
6. **Rising Danger** — investigation escalates; threats increase, relationships strain, PI's safety genuinely compromised
7. **Climactic Confrontation** — PI faces perpetrator, client, corrupt officials, or dangerous truth; moment of maximum pressure
8. **Gritty Resolution** — case closes but not cleanly; truth revealed but justice unclear; PI emerges changed but functional
