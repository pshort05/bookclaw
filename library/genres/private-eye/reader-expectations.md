Readers expect a narrative that delivers on specific emotional and structural promises.

## Tone

Readers expect Tone: Cynical, Atmospheric, Gritty, World-Weary

## Pacing

The pacing should be Tough Talk: Characters speak to dominate the space or hide their fear. Dialogue is confrontational.

## Setting

Tone: Cynical, Atmospheric, Gritty, World-Weary

## Archetypes

The Internal Monologue: The protagonist’s inner thoughts are as important as the dialogue. They should observe the world with detachment, irony, and pessimism.

## Length & Format

Standard novel length for this genre is 70,000–120,000 words.
