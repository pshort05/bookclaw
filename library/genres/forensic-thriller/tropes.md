- The Signature (The Ritual): The antagonist does not just kill; they curate. The crime scene is arranged, organs are taken, or symbols are left behind. The deviation from this pattern (or the escalation of it) drives the plot.

## Rising Tropes

- Fluids: Blood spatter patterns, luminol glowing blue in the dark, formaldehyde, bleach.
- Key Trope Variation: The Cold Case. The crime happened years ago. The clock isn't ticking toward a new murder, but toward the evidence vanishing forever.
- Key Trope Variation: The Digital Ghost. The victim’s social media posts continue after death, or the killer hides inside the protagonist’s computer.
- Key Trope Variation: Empathy as a Weapon. Instead of detached observation, the protagonist must become the killer in their mind to understand the crime scene arrangement.
- Key Trope Variation: The Skeptic vs. The Superstitious. The protagonist fights against a society that believes in curses or demons, using primitive science (fingerprinting, early ballistics) to prove human agency.
