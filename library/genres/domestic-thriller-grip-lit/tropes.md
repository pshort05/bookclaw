- Common Tropes
- Trope: The Chameleon. One spouse completely reinvented themselves to marry the other, hiding a criminal or dark past.
- Trope: Mutual Destruction. Both spouses are toxic; it is a game of cat-and-mouse where neither is the hero.

## Rising Tropes

- Trope: The Cuckoo in the Nest. An interloper (nanny, long-lost friend) enters the home and systematically dismantles the family to take the protagonist's place.
- Trope: Class Envy. The outsider despises the wealthy family they serve and seeks to destroy them out of resentment.
- Trope: The Queen Bee. A powerful, manipulative mother who rules the social circle and destroys anyone who crosses her.
- Trope: The Child Witness. A child knows the truth but stays silent due to fear, trauma, or misunderstanding.
- Trope: Munchausen by Proxy. A mother makes her child sick (or vice versa) to maintain dependence and gain sympathy.
