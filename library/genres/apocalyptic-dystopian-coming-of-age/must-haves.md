Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] **Act I:** Established the protagonist’s “cage” (rules, limits, daily struggle).
-  [ ] **Act I:** Showed a specific flaw in their worldview or situation.
-  [ ] **Act I:** A concrete, personal catalyst forces a reluctant choice to act.
-  [ ] **Act II:** The protagonist enters a new, more dangerous physical/moral landscape.
-  [ ] **Act II:** Each major encounter/ally/lesson has a clear cost or consequence.
-  [ ] **Act II:** A major setback occurs, directly tied to the protagonist’s remaining flaw.
-  [ ] **Act III:** Protagonist makes a conscious, defining choice about their identity/morals.
-  [ ] **Act III:** The final confrontation tests this new identity, not just their strength.
-  [ ] **Act III:** The ending shows the protagonist’s changed role in the ongoing world.
