- The "power of friendship" trope undergoes interesting treatment in dark fantasy, appearing in contexts where isolated, traumatized characters gradually learn to depend on others not for moral salvation but for practical survival and psychological healing. Rather than friendship providing magical power or moral clarity, it provides emotional grounding and mutual support in navigating impossible circumstances.
