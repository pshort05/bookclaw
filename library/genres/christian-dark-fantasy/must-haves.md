Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] Ordinary world established with spiritual baseline
-  [ ] Inciting incident introduces the darkness
-  [ ] Thematic question planted (implicit, not stated)
-  [ ] Protagonist's flaw or wound is visible
-  [ ] Threshold crossed: no return to former self
-  [ ] Darkness escalates in stages
-  [ ] Antagonist's full threat revealed
-  [ ] Protagonist's flaw exploited against them
-  [ ] Midpoint revelation recontextualizes the conflict
-  [ ] Allies tested; betrayals possible
-  [ ] False victories lull protagonist before deeper crisis
-  [ ] Full weight of antagonist's power brought to bear
-  [ ] Moral choices become irreversible
-  [ ] Spiritual stakes reach their apex
-  [ ] Protagonist must surrender, sacrifice, or break
-  [ ] Climax resolves through decision as much as action
-  [ ] Aftermath is honest—scars remain
-  [ ] World changed but not perfected
-  [ ] Hope walks forward wounded
-  [ ] Final image resonates with thematic question
