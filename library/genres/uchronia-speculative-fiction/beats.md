## Obligatory Scenes

-  [ ] **Setting:** Does the scene's location (a street, a salon, a factory) reflect the alternate history in its details?
-  [ ] **Character Goal:** What does the POV character want *here*, *now*, based on their understanding of this world?
-  [ ] **Obstacle:** Is the obstacle (another character, a societal rule, a technological limit) a product of the altered timeline?
-  [ ] **Dialogue:** Does the conversation reveal world-building, advance the plot, *and* sound appropriate to the characters' era/class?
-  [ ] **Sensory Detail:** Are there 1-2 specific period/alternate-history details perceived through smell, sound, or touch?
-  [ ] **Reaction:** Does the character's reaction to events/show their era-specific worldview?
-  [ ] **Plot Movement:** Does the scene's outcome change the situation and raise a new story question?
-  [ ] **Subtle Divergence:** Is there at least one small, unremarked-upon detail in the background that signifies this is not our world?
