Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] **Opening Scene:** Does it vividly showcase a key difference from our world?
-  [ ] **POD Clarity:** Is the Point of Divergence (or its primary consequence) established early and clearly?
-  [ ] **Protagonist's Goal:** Is their desire rooted in the conditions of the alternate world?
-  [ ] **Antagonist's Logic:** Does their opposition stem logically from the altered timeline's new status quo?
-  [ ] **Midpoint Revelation:** Does an event or discovery significantly raise the stakes or re-frame the conflict?
-  [ ] **Climax Logic:** Does the final confrontation hinge on the unique rules/history of the alternate world?
-  [ ] **Consequences Shown:** Does the ending illustrate the new equilibrium (for better or worse) established by the plot?
-  [ ] **Thematic Payoff:** Does the story offer a coherent perspective on its central "what if" question?
