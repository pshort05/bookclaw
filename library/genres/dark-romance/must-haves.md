# Dark Romance — Must-Haves (non-negotiable checklist)

Skip these and it isn't really dark/mafia romance. Treat as a revision pass.

- [ ] **A visceral, tangible power imbalance** — a stark contrast in wealth, physical threat, or underworld status that forces the heroine into the hero's orbit and sustains first-act friction.
- [ ] **Justified, high-stakes external conflict** — a rival syndicate, an unhinged stalker, or an inescapable blood debt providing relentless pressure; the romance cannot exist in a vacuum.
- [ ] **Targeted, unapologetic lethality** — the hero shows extreme violence and criminal dominance toward the world, contrasted by obsessive gentleness and loyalty toward the heroine. A villain to everyone but her.
- [ ] **Meaningful character agency in confinement** — even captive or subordinated, the heroine fights back, manipulates her environment, or shows resilience that forces the anti-hero's respect. No passive victims.
- [ ] **Trauma acknowledgment and integration** — the leads' dark pasts inform their present fears and choices; scars are validated and accepted, not magically cured. Intimacy built on shared brokenness.
- [ ] **The "scorched earth" retribution climax** — the hero uses his dark resources to annihilate the external threat, paying off the "Touch Her and Die" promise set in chapter one.
- [ ] **A guaranteed HEA or HFN** — the leads end together, victorious, relationship secure. Zero flexibility; the catharsis of a happy ending is the contract.
