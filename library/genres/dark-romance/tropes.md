- 1. Major Tropes
- Recurring plot devices, character dynamics, and narrative patterns.
- Run, Rabbit, Run: The "Primal Play" trope. The MMC gives the FMC a head start or encourages her to run so he can hunt her. Fear acts as an aphrodisiac for the MMC (and eventually the FMC).

## Rising Tropes

- Recurring imagery, sensory details, and structural elements.
- Key Trope Variation: Arranged Marriage. The union is for business/peace treaty. Love is an inconvenient afterthought.
- Key Trope Variation: I Know What's Best For You. The MMC manipulates the FMC’s life from the shadows (getting her fired, scaring off boyfriends) to isolate her so he can swoop in as the savior.
- Key Trope Variation: Public Humiliation. The torture isn't just physical; it's social. The MMC turns the school/town against the FMC.
- Key Trope Variation: The Stockholm Shift. The explicit transition from seeing the captor as the Jailer to seeing him as the Provider.
