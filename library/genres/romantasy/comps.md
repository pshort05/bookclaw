## Comparable Titles

- Strategic Mandate: The protagonist must dominate the narrative through a high-stakes combination of capability and hidden wounds. Their power is only as compelling as the trauma they are actively navigating.

- Components of the Morally Gray Lead
- The Competence Porn Beat: Include a specific scene where the characters are forced to witness and admire each other’s high-level skills or strategic brilliance. This builds the foundation of respect.
- Stylistic Mandate: Utilize First-person present as the primary prose style to maximize sensory immediacy and place the reader directly inside the emotional furnace of the romance.
- The Information Gap: Use the ML’s POV as a strategic tool to reveal information the reader knows (e.g., his secret feelings or political maneuvers) but the protagonist does not, building "dramatic irony" and romantic tension.
- Effective Romantasy distinguishes between "high-intensity" passion and harmful tropes that alienate modern readers.
- Is the love interest a complex archetype (Fae, Dragon, Vampire) with a unique history?
- Is the Enemies-to-Lovers arc earned through a "Competence Porn" beat?
