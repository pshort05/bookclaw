- Glitz and glamor romance represents one of the most indulgent and escapist subgenres within the broader romance fiction landscape, centered on the lives and loves of the wealthy elite and jet-set socialites. This subgenre emerged as a distinctive category of romance literature in the late twentieth century, evolving from earlier "bonkbuster" traditions to become a sophisticated exploration of luxury, power dynamics, and romantic connection among characters who inhabit worlds of extraordinary wealth and privilege. Unlike some romance subgenres that focus primarily on emotional intimacy or personal growth, glitz romance prioritizes the aesthetic and material dimensions of its characters' lives—their penthouses, yachts, designer wardrobes, exotic destinations, and the complex social hierarchies of the ultra-wealthy—while maintaining the genre's core commitment to romantic fulfillment and emotional resolution.
