Readers expect a narrative that delivers on specific emotional and structural promises.

## Tone

Readers expect Faster-Than-Light Communication: Systems to bridge cosmic distances with immediate effects.

## Pacing

The pacing should be Space Opera Science Fiction Lexicon

## Setting

Terraforming & Colonization: The creation and defense of habitable worlds.

## Archetypes

Heroic/Anti-Heroic Journey: Protagonists rising (or being forced) from obscurity to galaxy-shaping roles, whether classic heroes or morally ambiguous anti-heroes.

## Length & Format

Standard novel length for this genre is 70,000–120,000 words.
