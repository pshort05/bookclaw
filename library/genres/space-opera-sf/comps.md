## Comparable Titles

- Below is a comprehensive lexicon of Tropes, Themes, Motifs, and Symbols curated specifically for the Space Opera genre. This document is suitable for use as a reference or codex entry in a novel-planning tool like NovelCrafter.
- Interstellar Empire Building: The rise (or collapse) of massive galactic empires, often encompassing dynastic struggles.
- Rebellion Against Authority: Uprisings and resistance against oppressive regimes or status-quo powers.
- Legitimacy & Authority: What constitutes the right to rule.
- Swords & Blades: Authority, action, or legitimate violence.
- Keys (Access/Authority): Power to open, close, reveal.
