# LGBTQ+ Romance — Comparable Titles

- **Red, White & Royal Blue by Casey McQuiston** — contemporary rom-com excellence; coming out + political romance; perfect balance of humor and heart.
- **The Song of Achilles by Madeline Miller** — literary historical romance; fated connection; intimate dual POV across a mythic journey.
- **A Deadly Education by Naomi Novik** — dark academia with a queer love story; found family + danger; complex female friendships.
- **House of Hunger by Alexis Henderson** — gothic historical sapphic romance; atmospheric dark academia; lush, literary prose.
- **The Shadows Between Us by Tricia Levenseller** — paranormal/fantasy romance; dark, intense, morally grey characters; high-heat sapphic love.
- **Boyfriend Material by Alexis Hall** — contemporary rom-com perfection; fake dating to real love; banter and genuine emotional depth.
- **Swordheart by T. Kingfisher** — paranormal romance with queer secondary characters; deep emotional intimacy; found family.
