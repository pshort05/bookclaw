# Space Fantasy Romance — Themes

- **Fate & agency** — the tension between a destined connection and the lovers' autonomous choice to honor or resist it.
- **Power & vulnerability** — characters wielding immense cosmic power forced to be emotionally vulnerable with their lover.
- **Bridging divides** — love as a force that transcends species, culture, politics, and cosmic law.
- **The burden of destiny** — the weight of prophecy, duty, and cosmically significant stakes bearing down on an intimate relationship.
- **Ambition & loyalty** — balancing personal political ambitions with loyalty to a lover or chosen family.
- **Transformation through connection** — the lovers changing each other fundamentally; the relationship remakes them.
