# Space Fantasy Romance — Tropes

- **Fated mates** — a magical or prophesied connection between lovers; the bond is cosmically destined.
- **Political marriage / marriage of convenience** — two people from different star systems, species, or factions bonded through politics; feelings develop from proximity and necessity.
- **Enemies to lovers (inter-species conflict)** — characters from opposing cosmic powers forced to work together; attraction emerges from mutual respect and shared danger.
- **Deadly trials / magical quests** — tests that prove the lovers' bond and force them to reveal their deepest vulnerabilities.
- **Forbidden love (broken taboo)** — a relationship that violates species hierarchy, political law, or cosmic law; the romance becomes an act of rebellion.
- **Dragon riders / bonded magical creatures** — the lovers' magical bonds intertwine with or parallel their romantic connection.
- **Sacrifice & protection** — one lover willingly risking everything (power, status, life) for the other.
- **Reunion across worlds** — separated lovers navigating cosmic distances and political barriers to find each other again.
