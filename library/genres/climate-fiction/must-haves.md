Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] **Act I:** Established a *fractured* status quo that shows daily life under climate stress.
-  [ ] **Inciting Incident:** A second disaster or shocking truth breaks the protagonist's personal adaptation.
-  [ ] **Act II:** Journey/Confrontation explores different ideologies (tech-fix, regression, exploitation).
-  [ ] **Midpoint:** A seeming victory reveals a larger, more sinister systemic truth.
-  [ ] **All is Lost:** An environmental/personal catastrophe strips the protagonist of their last refuge/hope.
-  [ ] **Act III:** Climax revolves around a meaningful **choice** (not fixing the planet).
-  [ ] **Ending:** New equilibrium shows irreversible change; tone matches thematic argument (hopeful, tragic, resilient).
