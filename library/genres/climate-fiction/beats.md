## Obligatory Scenes

-  [ ] **Environment as Agent:** Does the weather/geography actively hinder, help, or change the scene's direction?
-  [ ] **Sensory Detail:** Have I invoked at least two non-visual senses (sound, smell, touch, taste)?
-  [ ] **Resource Reality:** Are the characters aware of/limited by resources (water, power, breathable air, calories)?
-  [ ] **Ideological Undercurrent:** Does the dialogue or conflict reveal differing views on the crisis?
-  [ ] **Past-Present Contrast:** Is there a subtle hint (a memory, a ruin, a changed tradition) of what was lost?
-  [ ] **Character Agency:** Is the protagonist making a choice, however small, rather than just reacting?
-  [ ] **Thematic Glance:** Does this scene, in some way, touch the core theme (e.g., justice, sacrifice, resilience)?
