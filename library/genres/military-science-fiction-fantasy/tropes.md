Tropes are the recurring patterns that readers expect and enjoy in this genre.

## Dominant Tropes

- Military Progression Fantasy**:
-  Explicit progression gates (skills unlock, rank requirements met)
-  Training sequences showing improvement
-  Skill mastery climaxes demonstrating advancement
-  Measurement of growth visible to reader

## Rising Tropes

- Grimdark Military**:
-  Non-linear narrative or multiple timelines revealing truth gradually
-  Moral compromises accumulating toward reckoning
-  Subverted victory scenarios
-  Character guilt and consequences extending across books
