## Rising Tropes

- Key Trope: The Near Miss. The two potential lovers pass each other constantly in the timeline where they don't meet, teasing the reader.
- Key Trope: The Perfect Day. The protagonist spends loops memorizing the LI's schedule to create a "perfect" romance, only to realize manipulation isn't love.
- Key Trope: The False Positive. The system says they aren't a match, but they fall in love anyway (Humanity vs. System).
- Key Trope: The Villainess. The protagonist wakes up as the antagonist of the story and must change their fate to avoid execution and find love.
- Key Trope: The Constant Variable. In infinite universes, the LI is the only thing that remains consistent.
