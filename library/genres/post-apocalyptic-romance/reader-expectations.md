## Pacing

The pacing should be No Cheating: In a world of billions of threats, the partner must be the one safe space.

## Setting

Trust as Currency: In a lawless world, giving someone your trust is more dangerous and valuable than food or ammo. Betrayal is a fate worse than death.

## Archetypes

Survival vs. Living: The central thematic conflict. "Survival" is merely not dying; "Living" involves joy, love, and future-building. The romance is usually the catalyst that moves the protagonist from the former to the latter.

## Length & Format

Standard novel length for this genre is 70,000–120,000 words.
