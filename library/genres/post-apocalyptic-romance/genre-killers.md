*Avoid these patterns—they undermine the hard-won authenticity post-apocalyptic romance demands.*

- ✗ **Softcore Apocalypse** — danger is background; protagonists maintain pre-collapse comfort and convenience; undermines survival stakes
- ✗ **Love Solves Survival** — romance somehow enables resource abundance or protection; love can't substitute for practical survival skills
- ✗ **One-Sided Rescue** — one protagonist constantly saves other; undermines reciprocal partnership survival romance requires
- ✗ **Instant Intimacy** — protagonists fall in love immediately without trust-building through shared hardship; intimacy feels unearned
- ✗ **Ignored Trauma** — survivors experiencing no psychological impact from apocalypse; PTSD, grief, or survival-response stress invisible
- ✗ **Romance Trivializes Apocalypse** — love subplot takes precedence over survival; diminishes catastrophe's weight and stakes
- ✗ **Convenient Resource Access** — love tested by scarcity but never truly going without; survival stakes undercut by convenient solutions
- ✗ **Generic Diversity** — diverse protagonists but apocalypse affects them identically; misses how systemic inequity persists through collapse
