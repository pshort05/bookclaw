*Post-apocalyptic romance relies on separation-and-reunion and love deepened through survival collaboration.*

- **The Survival Partners** — two people thrust together by catastrophe; mutual protection evolves into romantic connection
- **The Lost/Separated Lovers** — protagonist separated from loved one during collapse; journey to reunite drives narrative and proves devotion worth
- **The Last People** — protagonists among final survivors; isolation intensifies emotional bonding and forces partnership focus
- **Competing Loyalties** — protagonist must choose between love and group survival; loyalty conflicts create emotional tension
- **The Resource Dilemma** — love tested by scarcity; protagonists must decide what they're willing to sacrifice for each other
- **The Safe Haven** — couple works toward sanctuary together; shared goal and community-building deepen romantic bond
- **The Protection Instinct** — protagonists defend each other against threats; willingness to die for love tested by realistic danger
- **The Hope in Darkness** — romance becomes emotional survival; connection to loved one provides meaning when civilization collapses
