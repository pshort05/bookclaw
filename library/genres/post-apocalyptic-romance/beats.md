*Post-apocalyptic romance typically parallels survival progress with relationship deepening.*

1. **Collapse and Separation/Meeting** — apocalypse occurs; protagonists either become separated or meet for first time amid chaos
2. **Survival Alliance** — necessity forces partnership; protagonists must collaborate for mutual survival despite differences or initial tension
3. **Dependence and Trust-Building** — working together, learning each other's strengths, developing mutual reliance; trust incrementally deepens
4. **First Real Connection** — moment of genuine intimacy (emotional vulnerability, physical closeness, or revelation) transforms partnership from practical to romantic
5. **Conflict Tests Commitment** — external threat or resource scarcity creates conflict forcing choice about willingness to sacrifice for partner
6. **Shared Goal/Sanctuary** — couple works toward common objective (reaching safe haven, building shelter, protecting found community) that strengthens bond
7. **Crisis/Separation** — major threat, injury, or loss temporarily separates protagonists or forces impossible choice
8. **Reunion and Resolution** — protagonists reunite/choose each other explicitly; future together in post-collapse world established
