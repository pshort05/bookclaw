## Failure Modes

- ## 5. KILLER OPENING FORMULA (First 500 words)
-  **AVOID THIS**: Don't introduce romantic subplots until after midpoint (breaks "lone grinder" trope)—early ones dilute focus, per BookBub survey complaints on diluted progression.
-  **Critical Detail**: The system doesn't have to be stat-heavy (numbers don't dominate), but it must follow **internal rules readers can predict**. Arbitrary power-ups = DNF
-  Another: "Skills cost stamina, except when they don't. No explanation. Makes every fight feel arbitrary."
-  **The Fix**: Let smart MCs make tactical errors (miscalculation, incomplete info, moral dilemma) rather than logical failures.
