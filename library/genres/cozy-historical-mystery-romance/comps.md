## Comparable Titles

- Definition: The central romantic relationship develops through the shared endeavor of solving the mystery, built on mutual respect, complementary skills, and witty banter.
- Variations: Regency focuses on etiquette and marriage mart. Victorian focuses on class and morality. Medieval focuses on feudal duty and religious authority.
- Reader Consumption Habits Report
- This readership demonstrates a strong dual-format preference, but with a notable tilt toward physical books compared to many other fiction genres.
- Reading Volume & Speed
- Readers in this niche are high-volume, binge-oriented consumers.
- Average Monthly Reads: 4–8 books per month.
- Binge-Reading Behavior: Extremely common. Once a series is discovered, readers will often consume 3–5 books in quick succession.
- Annual Volume: Estimated 50–100 books per year, placing them well above the average reader’s consumption rate.
- Kindle Unlimited (KU): Dominant platform. An estimated 70–80% of avid readers in this genre use KU regularly.
