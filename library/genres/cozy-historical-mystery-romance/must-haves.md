Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] Introduce protagonist and setting with historical context.
-  [ ] Inciting incident: a cozy mystery (e.g., theft, disappearance) occurs.
-  [ ] Establish romantic interest with initial chemistry or conflict.
-  [ ] Protagonist investigates, gathering clues and interacting with suspects.
-  [ ] Romantic subplot develops through shared moments and dialogue.
-  [ ] Climax: mystery solved, often with romantic realization or challenge.
-  [ ] Resolution: mystery resolved justly, romance affirmed, community restored.
