Avoid these mistakes that can undermine your story.

## Failure Modes

- Pacing: Sentences should be shorter and punchier during action sequences. Use active verbs. Avoid passive voice.
- Don't Pause for Monologues: Do not have characters stop to confess their feelings in the middle of an active firefight unless they are pinned down.
- Trust vs. Survival: In a world of spies and killers, paranoia keeps you alive. Love requires vulnerability. The arc involves unlearning survival instincts to let someone in.
- The symbolic power of sacrifice—characters choosing love over career advancement, security, or self-preservation—proves central to action romance's emotional resolution. Happy endings don't merely resolve external conflicts; they demonstrate that characters have fundamentally reorganized their priorities, placing romantic partnership above professional achievement or personal ambition.[7][25][12]
