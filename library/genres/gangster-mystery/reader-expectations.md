## Setting

The Gangster Mystery is a potent hybrid genre where the brutal, hierarchical world of organized crime collides with the investigative, revelatory mechanics of a mystery. The central tension often lies not in who committed a crime—that’s usually understood—but in why, how it will be resolved, and what secrets the criminal world is desperate to keep buried.
