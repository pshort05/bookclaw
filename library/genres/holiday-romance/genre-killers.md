## Failure Modes

- Common Pitfalls — Ten key mistakes to avoid
- Note: Multiple POVs are acceptable but should alternate clearly; avoid head-hopping within scenes
- Accessible and conversational (avoid overwrought prose)
- Avoid:
- The Fake Dating Fiasco: To avoid family nagging about being single during the holidays, the protagonists agree to pretend to be a couple until New Year’s. Real feelings inevitably develop.
- Key Trope Variation: Christmas under Siege. Bad guys take over the office party or the blizzard traps a killer with them.
- Light & Breezy: The prose should be accessible and inviting. Avoid dense, heavy, or overly dark introspection.
- No "Grim" Reality: Avoid politics, gore, intense trauma, or explicit cruelty. This is escapism.
