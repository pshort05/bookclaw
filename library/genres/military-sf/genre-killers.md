## Failure Modes

- Objective & Economical: Use active verbs and concrete nouns. Avoid flowery metaphors. Describe scenes with the precision of an after-action report.
- Lethality: Combat is fast and brutal. Armor degrades, shields fail, and injuries are visceral. Avoid "hero armor"—consequences must feel real.
