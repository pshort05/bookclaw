Core themes and ideas central to this genre.

## Central Themes

- COMFORT + CREEPINESS = COZY HORROR**
-  Primary emotion: Whimsy and dread simultaneously
-  Reader expectation: Safe scares and emotional satisfaction
-  Tone: Warm, funny, mysterious, never traumatizing
-  Stakes: Personal and localized, never apocalyptic
-  Resolution: Satisfying emotional closure, always
- | Element | Cozy Horror ✓ | Cozy Horror ✗ |
- |---------|---------------|---------------|
- | Horror Level | Atmospheric, uncanny, mysterious | Graphic, visceral, traumatic |
- | Violence | Implied, offstage, minimal | Explicit, graphic, gory |
- | Ending | Satisfying, emotionally resolved | Bleak, ambiguous, tragic |
- | Scope | Localized, personal | Apocalyptic, world-threatening |
- | Community | Central to story | Peripheral or absent |
- | Humor | Regular, structural | Occasional, decorative |
- | Protagonist | Agentic, brave, curious | Helpless, passive, victimized |
- | Supernatural Beings | Complex, often sympathetic | Purely evil or threatening |
