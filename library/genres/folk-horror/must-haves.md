## Essential Checklist

- Use this as a quick pass after outlining or drafting.
-  [ ] Clear **reason to come** (inheritance, research, job, relationship, accident).
-  [ ] The place is **welcoming but odd**: too quiet, too watchful, too uniform.
-  [ ] A **local story or warning** appears (tale, song, carved figure).[cite:31]
-  [ ] A **boundary** is crossed (bridge, stile, sign, gate, social vow).
-  [ ] Protagonist is **drawn into daily life** (help with harvest, repairs, festival prep).
-  [ ] Social obligations accumulate (favors owed, hospitality, romance, partnership).
-  [ ] First **public ritual or festival** occurs; something is subtly wrong.
-  [ ] A **taboo** is broken (knowingly or not), with a minor but ominous consequence.
-  [ ] Hidden space or record reveals the true **history/cost** (journal, shrine, pit, stone circle).
-  [ ] Attempts to leave are blocked (no transport, storms, threats, emotional blackmail).
-  [ ] The **logic of the belief system** becomes clear enough to force a choice.
-  [ ] Allies prove unreliable or compromised.
-  [ ] Central ritual or confrontation enacts the community’s **bargain with the land**.
-  [ ] Protagonist chooses: submit, rebel, or subvert within the system.
-  [ ] Outcome feels inevitable given earlier symbols and rules.
-  [ ] Final image suggests **cycle, corruption, or ambiguous change**.
