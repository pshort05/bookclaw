## Obligatory Scenes

-  [ ] **Goal:** Does the POV character have a clear goal for the scene?
-  [ ] **Setting:** Is the scene anchored by at least two historical sensory details (e.g., smell of incense, sound of sandals on mosaic)?
-  [ ] **Worldview:** Does the dialogue and internal thought reflect the ancient mindset (gods, fate, honor)?
-  [ ] **The Anomaly:** Is the sci-fi element being viewed, described, or interacted with through a historical lens?
-  [ ] **Conflict & Advancement:** Does the scene contain conflict and either advance the plot or reveal crucial character information?
-  [ ] **Hook:** Does the scene end on a question, a decision, or a new complication that makes the reader want to turn the page?
