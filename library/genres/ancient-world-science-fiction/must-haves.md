## Essential Checklist

- Use this to check your plot beats.
-  [ ] **The Mundane:** Have you established the protagonist's normal life in the ancient world?
-  [ ] **The Anomaly:** Has the sci-fi element been introduced in a way that disrupts that normal life?
-  [ ] **The Hook:** Is the protagonist personally invested or forced into dealing with the anomaly?
-  [ ] **Investigation:** Do characters attempt to understand the anomaly, with mixed results?
-  [ ] **Escalation:** Does the anomaly's influence grow, causing wider societal impact?
-  [ ] **Factionalism:** Have groups formed around the anomaly (Worship, Control, Destroy)?
-  [ ] **Midpoint Shift:** Does a major event dramatically raise the stakes and change the game?
-  [ ] **The Low Point:** Does the antagonist/opposing force gain the upper hand?
-  [ ] **The Final Plan:** Does the protagonist synthesize their old knowledge with the new to form a plan?
-  [ ] **Climax:** Is there a final, decisive confrontation over the control/fate of the anomaly?
-  [ ] **The New Normal:** Does the resolution show how the world has been permanently altered by the events?
