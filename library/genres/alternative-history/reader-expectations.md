## Setting

Alternative history fiction (also called alternate history, allohistory, or althist) is a speculative fiction subgenre that asks "What if?" by altering one or more historical events and exploring the consequences. It combines historical accuracy with imaginative speculation to create worlds that are both familiar and dramatically different.
