## Central Themes

- Themes (Underlying Philosophies)
- Cyclical History: The idea that humanity eventually outpaces its morality, collapses, and returns to a dark age, repeating the cycle.
- Variation: Technology didn't "fall"; it evolved differently. The motif is Mechanical Complexity (gears, brass, springs). The theme is often the Hubris of Discovery during a Renaissance-style awakening.
- Definition and Core Characteristics
- The idea that a medieval society exists in the ruins of a much more advanced civilization.
