Readers expect Reliquaries of Circuitry: Circuit boards kept in ornate gold boxes; wires draped like ivy over stone

## Setting

The Wasteland The area outside the "Castle" (dome/city), representing the chaos of a collapsed world.

## Archetypes

Variation: Technology is often "lost" or alien. The emphasis is on The Hero’s Journey and exoticism. The "Medieval" aspect is usually the social hierarchy (Emperors/Princesses) and the focus on hand-to-hand combat due to local cultural taboos or environmental constraints.

## Length & Format

Standard novel length for this genre is 70,000–120,000 words.
