*Portal SF relies on technology-enabled world contact and the vast consequences when distant realities become accessible.*

- **The Accidental Breach** — scientist/explorer accidentally opens portal between worlds; unintended consequences cascade from this single mistake
- **The Experimental Gateway** — technology developed to deliberately create stable portal; unintended contact with unexpected worlds or civilizations
- **The Ancient Portal Network** — discovery that portals existed anciently; exploration of which advanced civilization built them and why
- **The Dimensional Invaders** — hostile beings from parallel dimension use portal technology for invasion; military/defensive response required
- **The Resource Competition** — multiple worlds accessible through portal create economic conflicts; factions compete for resource access and control
- **The Temporal Portal** — portal connects to past or future; time travel paradoxes and linear history disruption complicate narrative
- **The Cultural Shock** — worlds with vastly different technological development/values come into contact through portal; exchange/conflict unavoidable
- **The Physics Breakdown** — portal technology violates previously understood laws of physics; scientists must reconsider reality's fundamental nature
