Readers expect a narrative that delivers on specific emotional and structural promises.

## Tone

Readers expect Global Narrative Voice

## Pacing

The pacing should be Tense: Past Tense (standard) or Present Tense (for thriller/survival pacing).

## Setting

Environmental Contrast: Contrast the sterile, metallic, controlled environment of the "Gate Room" or "Lab" with the chaotic, alien, or biological nature of the destination.

## Archetypes

Narrative Focus: Problem-solving and exploration. The characters encounter the impossible, but they try to explain it through logic, math, and physics rather than accepting it as magic.

## Length & Format

Standard novel length for this genre is 70,000–120,000 words.
