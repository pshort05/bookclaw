*Portal science fiction typically follows portal discovery through multi-world consequences and political/scientific reckonings.*

1. **Scientific Breakthrough** — researcher/explorer makes discovery enabling portal creation or accidental breach opens
2. **Portal Activation/Opening** — gateway successfully opens to unexpected world; early contact/exploration begins
3. **Initial Contact** — meeting inhabitants or entities on other side; establishing communication (or discovering communication barriers)
4. **Unintended Consequences Emerge** — portal contact creates unexpected problems: invasion threat, resource depletion, cultural disruption, physics violations
5. **Political/Military Response** — governments/factions vie for portal control; stakes escalate as multiple interests pursue access
6. **Crisis Point** — portal malfunction, invasion, or revelation threatens catastrophe; all-or-nothing moment requiring decisive action
7. **Confrontation/Resolution** — climactic engagement either closes portal, negotiates multi-world treaty, or accepts new status-quo
8. **New Reality Accepted** — ending establishes changed relationship with portal-connected worlds; implications for humanity's future
