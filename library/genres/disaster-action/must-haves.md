Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] **Act I:** Normal world established with protagonist's flaw/strength.
-  [ ] **Act I:** Foreshadowing of disaster (subtle or direct).
-  [ ] **Inciting Incident:** First major disaster strike.
-  [ ] **Act II:** Immediate survival sequence (chaos, escape).
-  [ ] **Act II:** Clear survival goal established (find family, reach safety).
-  [ ] **Midpoint:** Major setback/complication raises stakes.
-  [ ] **Act II:** Antagonists emerge (human or disaster-driven).
-  [ ] **Act II:** "The Long Haul" – exhaustion, tough choices.
-  [ ] **Act III:** Final, climactic confrontation with disaster.
-  [ ] **Act III:** Sacrifice or key skill application resolves climax.
-  [ ] **Act III:** Aftermath shows the cost.
-  [ ] **Act III:** Brief glimpse of the "new normal."
