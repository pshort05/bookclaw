# Space Romance — Themes

- **Isolation & connection** — the vastness of space amplifying the intensity of intimate human connection.
- **Survival & interdependence** — love emerging from mutual dependence and the need to trust each other.
- **Professional excellence under pressure** — characters maintaining competence and respect even as romantic feelings escalate.
- **The frontier as escape** — space as a place to start over, shed the past, or pursue dreams.
- **Humanity & vulnerability in the cosmos** — stripping away the safety of civilization forces authentic emotional connection.
- **Community in isolation** — chosen family and bonds forged through shared danger and isolation.
