# Space Romance — Tropes

- **Forced proximity (isolated spacecraft)** — two people trapped together on a ship, station, or colony; isolation forces emotional intimacy.
- **Rivals to lovers (competing missions)** — two space professionals competing for the same objective or resources; tension morphs into attraction.
- **Rescue / survival romance** — a dangerous situation (system failure, crash landing, hostile environment) forcing characters to depend on each other emotionally and physically.
- **Enemies working together (different factions)** — characters from opposing space-based organizations forced to collaborate; tension and attraction.
- **Second chance at love (reunion in space)** — former lovers unexpectedly reunited in a space setting; unresolved feelings + isolated environment.
- **The outsider & the veteran** — a newcomer to space work paired with an experienced space professional; mentorship + attraction.
- **Communication delays & longing** — romantic connection strained by the physical vastness of space and communication lag.
- **Found family (ship's crew)** — the crew as chosen family; bonds deepen through shared danger and isolation.
