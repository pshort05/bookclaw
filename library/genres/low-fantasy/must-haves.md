Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

- Act 1: The Gritty Normal & Disruption**
-  [ ] Establish the protagonist’s flawed, grounded life.
-  [ ] Show the realistic social/political world.
-  [ ] Introduce the personal or local conflict (not world-spanning).
-  [ ] Include only hints, rumors, or one faint magical oddity.
-  [ ] End with a decision or event that forces the protagonist into the conflict.
- Act 2: The Costly Pursuit**
-  [ ] Protagonist makes morally ambiguous alliances/choices.
-  [ ] Political/social intrigue deepens.
-  [ ] Include ONE significant, disturbing magical encounter or discovery.
-  [ ] Show the physical, social, or psychological cost of the pursuit.
-  [ ] A major betrayal or reversal occurs.
- Act 3: The Bitter Resolution**
-  [ ] Climax focuses on human confrontation (fight, negotiation, exposure).
-  [ ] Magic, if used, is costly and has unintended consequences.
-  [ ] The primary goal is achieved at great loss, or failed, leaving change.
-  [ ] No restoration of a perfect world.
-  [ ] Ending focuses on the protagonist’s altered state.
