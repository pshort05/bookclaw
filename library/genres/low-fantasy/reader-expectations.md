## Setting

Low Fantasy is a subgenre of fantasy where magical or supernatural elements are intrusive into, and often disruptive of, a recognizably realistic, mundane world. The focus is typically on character drama, moral ambiguity, and survival within a gritty, grounded setting where magic is rare, dangerous, costly, or unreliable.
