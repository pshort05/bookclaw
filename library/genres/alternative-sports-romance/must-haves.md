# Alternative Sports Romance — Must-Haves (non-negotiable checklist)

Skip these and the manuscript fails the fundamental requirements of the genre. Treat as a
revision pass.

- [ ] **Authentic sport mechanics** — the specific rules, culture, training rhythms, and competitive structure of this discipline must be accurate and grounded. Generic "sports" language breaks the reader contract; soccer has different emotional texture than rugby which is nothing like rock climbing.
- [ ] **Athletic identity as non-negotiable** — the protagonist's relationship with their sport is not a hobby or a backdrop; it is their identity. The sport cannot be discarded, minimized, or solved away by the romance. Both must coexist at the end.
- [ ] **Dual-arc escalation** — romantic tension and competitive stakes must escalate in parallel. Every chapter that advances romance should also advance athletic stakes, and vice versa. A romance arc floating above a static sports plot fails the genre.
- [ ] **Respect between competitors** — even rivals must demonstrate genuine respect for each other's athletic excellence. The lead may resent the other's presence in their lane but cannot doubt their ability.
- [ ] **Embodied physicality** — athletes experience the world through their bodies; training pain, performance highs, physical exhaustion, and sport-conditioned strength are present on the page, not filtered out for modesty.
- [ ] **HEA/HFN without career destruction** — neither lead abandons their athletic ambitions to win the relationship. If an athlete retires in the resolution, it must be their own autonomous choice, not a sacrifice coerced by the romance.
- [ ] **Sport-rooted conflict source** — the external conflict driving the black moment must originate inside the sport world (selection politics, rival sponsorship, injury, coach pressure, team loyalty) — not a coincidence or a generic miscommunication unrelated to athletic life.
- [ ] **The adhesion has sporting logic** — the mechanism forcing the leads together (shared team, same training program, mandatory partnership, concurrent injury rehab) must arise naturally from how this specific sport operates.
- [ ] **Physical competence is attractive** — the attraction is grounded in watching the other person excel at something difficult and demanding. This is the genre's unique charge; do not elide the athletic admiration in favor of purely conventional romantic beats.
