# Alternative Sports Romance — Tropes

- **Rivals to lovers** — two athletes competing for the same opportunity, prize, or title; tension morphs into attraction.
- **Teammates / forced proximity** — shared training, travel, team dynamics force continuous intimate interaction.
- **Coach × athlete (power-balanced)** — mentorship where the coach respects the athlete's autonomy and agency.
- **Injury recovery & vulnerability** — career-threatening injury forces emotional openness and dependence.
- **Second-chance athletes** — former competitors reuniting in the professional arena years later.
- **Cross-discipline attraction** — athletes from different sports or different competitive levels navigating romance.
- **Found family (athletic community)** — teammates and the athletic community as emotional support enriching the romance.
- **The breakthrough** — an athlete achieving a long-sought goal while falling in love; balancing ambition and connection.
