# Alternative Sports Romance — Themes

- **Athletic excellence as identity** — the sport and the athlete's competence are non-negotiable to the character and story.
- **Respect for niche athletic cultures** — each sport has distinct demands, communities, and value systems; celebrate what makes each unique.
- **Balancing competitive drive with partnership** — showing how love and high-level athletic ambition can coexist and strengthen each other.
- **Physical power & bodily autonomy** — celebrating strength, skill, and a capable body.
- **Underdog narratives in overlooked disciplines** — finding heroism and excellence in sports that receive less mainstream visibility.
- **Community & camaraderie** — the team and athletic community as a source of belonging and support.
