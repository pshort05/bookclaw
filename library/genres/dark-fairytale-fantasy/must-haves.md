Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] **Act I:** A flawed protagonist in a grim-but-stable ordinary world.
-  [ ] The "Call" is a tangible, dark threat (theft, curse, sickness).
-  [ ] Refusal is based on a understandable, personal cost/fear.
-  [ ] The Ordinary World is shattered, forcing the journey.
-  [ ] **Act II:** Encounters test the protagonist's morals, not just their strength.
-  [ ] A pivotal, costly **Bargain** is made at the Midpoint.
-  [ ] The "Reward" after the central Ordeal is flawed or reveals a greater threat.
-  [ ] **Act III:** The climax is a **moral/psychological reckoning**, not just a fight.
-  [ ] The resolution is **bittersweet or cautiously hopeful**; scars and costs remain.
-  [ ] The ending reflects the protagonist's internal transformation.
