*These elements rapidly destroy reader investment; avoid them or reframe completely.*

- **Uncritical Yakuza Romanticism** — treating yakuza as noble outlaws without examining brutality, exploitation, and real human cost; modern readers skeptical of uncritical admiration; balance romanticism with moral reckoning
- **One-Dimensional Villains or Moral Simplicity** — yakuza/triad fiction requires moral complexity; straightforward good-vs-evil framing breaks genre contract and alienates contemporary audiences
- **Female Characters as Mere Rewards** — positioning women as prizes for male protagonists or love interests without agency/complexity; modern market demands complex female characters with independent motivations
- **Purely Male-Centered Hierarchies Without Interrogation** — traditional male-only yakuza increasingly challenged; if excluding female perspectives, must explore that exclusion critically rather than as unquestioned norm
- **Magical Escape Resolution** — avoid protagonist cleanly escaping criminal life with no psychological cost; yakuza fiction requires that escape is complex, incomplete, or impossible; trauma persists
- **Exoticization of Japanese/Chinese Culture** — treating cultural elements as exotic flavor rather than integral to character psychology and organizational logic; stereotyping, orientalism, or superficial cultural references alienate informed readers
- **Inaccurate Police Procedure or Legal Consequence** — if including law enforcement or legal system, get procedure right; shortcuts undermine credibility; audience includes readers with criminal justice expertise
- **Unmotivated Violence** — violence must serve psychological or plot function; gratuitous brutality without character or thematic purpose reads as exploitation
- **Protagonist Devoid of Internal Life** — yakuza fiction requires psychological depth; flat, action-only protagonists lack the complexity readers now expect; explore trauma, moral degradation, psychological cost
- **Missing Cultural Philosophy** — yakuza without jingi, triads without yi qi, characters without philosophical anchors feel hollow; organizational codes drive motivation and conflict; omit them and character logic collapses
- **Flat or Stereotypical Yakuza Members** — treat other organization members as complex characters with motivations, histories, internal conflicts; avoid cardboard henchmen or one-note archetypes
