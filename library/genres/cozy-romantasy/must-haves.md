Every book in this genre needs these essential elements.

## Essential Checklist

-  [ ] **Opening:** Protagonist's cozy, magical normal is established.
-  [ ] **Inciting Spark:** Love interest arrives/meets in a low-stakes, charming way.
-  [ ] **Forced Proximity:** A minor problem or community event forces them together.
-  [ ] **Warming Up:** Shared small successes & integration into each other's worlds.
-  [ ] **Intimacy Milestone:** A quiet moment of deep connection (by a fire, sharing a secret).
-  [ ] **Internal Conflict:** Protagonist's fear/wound surfaces, creating doubt.
-  [ ] **Cozy Dark Moment:** A misunderstanding leads to emotional separation; the warmth fades.
-  [ ] **Realization:** Each protagonist, aided by friends, understands their mistake/flaw.
-  [ ] **Grand Gesture:** A personal, meaningful act that speaks directly to the other's heart.
-  [ ] **Emotional Resolution:** Honest talk, acceptance, healing.
-  [ ] **HEA/HFN:** Final scene showing their joyful, cozy future together with their community.
