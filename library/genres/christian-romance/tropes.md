Tropes are the recurring patterns that readers expect and enjoy in this genre.

## Dominant Tropes

-  Second Chance Romance (with faith redemption arc)
-  Enemies to Lovers (divided faith becoming unified conviction)
-  Hidden Identity (often involving faith/past)
-  Small Town Romance (church-centered community)
-  Faith-Based Healing & Redemption

## Rising Tropes

-  Forced Proximity (faith-sharing scenarios: mission trip, retreat, church volunteer work)
-  **Narrative Structure:** Three-act structure with spiritual turning point at 75% mark
-  **Conflict Origin:** Must be faith-rooted; external obstacles support but never replace spiritual tension
-  **Pacing:** Often slower burn; character introspection takes time; emotional intimacy develops before physical
-  **Voice:** Introspective, authentic, hopeful; shows faith struggle without preaching
