- III.1 Common Themes and Motifs
- Totalitarianism and Resistance: Fascism, authoritarian control, and resistance movements recur throughout the genre. Even non-explicitly-political dieselpunk often explores themes of freedom versus control.
- Theme: The Ethics of Collaboration
- Decopunk (Strong aesthetic appeal, growing reader demand, relatively few published titles compared to core dieselpunk)
- Core Piecraftian Dieselpunk (Original market segment; well-established)
- Found Family and Queer Kinship: Increasingly central to reader preferences. Not just romance but deep exploration of chosen families and non-traditional support networks.
