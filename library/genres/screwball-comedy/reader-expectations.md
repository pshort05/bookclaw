Readers expect a narrative that delivers on specific emotional and structural promises.

## Tone

Readers expect Tone: Kinetic, Witty, Zany, Irreverent, Fast-Paced

## Pacing

The pacing should be Tone: Kinetic, Witty, Zany, Irreverent, Fast-Paced

## Setting

The Night in Jail: The chaotic events lead to the couple being arrested. The cell becomes a forced proximity setting for an argument or a reconciliation.

## Archetypes

Zero Lag: Internal monologues should be short. The focus is on reaction, not contemplation. Characters act first and think later (or never).

## Length & Format

Standard novel length for this genre is 70,000–120,000 words.
