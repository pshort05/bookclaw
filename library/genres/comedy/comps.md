## Comparable Titles

- Master/Veteran Character**: Speaks efficiently, uses industry jargon naturally, humor is dry and self-assured.
-  *"That's impossible." "So is most of Tuesday."*
- Skilled but Inexperienced Character**: Uses some jargon, humor is self-conscious or nervous.
-  *"I'm not sure about the extraction plan." "Neither am I, which is why we're really winging it."*
- Civilian/Newcomer**: No jargon, humor is reactive and confused.
-  *"What's a perimeter?" "The place we're not supposed to cross." "Oh. I crossed it."*
- 
- Act I: Setup & Inciting Incident (10-15% of novel)**
-  Introduce protagonist(s) and their world
-  Establish the tone through humor and character interaction
