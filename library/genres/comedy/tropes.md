## Rising Tropes

- 4.1 Dominant Tropes (Established and Popular)
- Mismatched Partnership: Two protagonists with conflicting personalities, skills, or backgrounds forced into collaboration. Foundational to buddy cop, spy, and heist subgenres. Market position: High demand, high saturation. Over 65% of action-comedy properties feature this trope.
- 4.2 Tropes on the Rise
- Self-Aware Meta-Humor: Protagonists demonstrating awareness of genre conventions, action-movie tropes, or narrative structure. Popularized by Deadpool; increasingly adopted across properties. Requires sophisticated audience understanding.
