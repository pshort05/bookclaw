# Voice Profile — Contemporary Thriller

A propulsive, transparent commercial-thriller voice: urgent, confident, and lightly
"intellectual" without slowing down. The narration grants the reader insider authority —
the sense of being briefed on how a real institution actually works — while keeping the
foot on the accelerator. Tension is the baseline register; competence and forward motion
are the relief.

Cool under pressure rather than purple: the voice reports catastrophe with clipped control
and lets the stakes, not the adjectives, supply the dread. Closer to the clean, readable
register of a present-day espionage or political thriller than to ornate literary prose —
every sentence earns its place by moving the story.
