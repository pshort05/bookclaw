# Voice Profile — Military Romantasy

A high-intensity, deeply interior romantasy voice: visceral, sensory, and emotionally raw, yet
sharpened by tactical precision. The narration thinks like someone trained to read a room and
control it — and then keeps betraying the feeling underneath that control. Yearning is the
baseline register; competence is the armor the story keeps cracking.

Concrete and embodied rather than abstract: every emotion lands somewhere in the body, every
intimate moment is grounded in texture and scent. Dual-POV close third (or first) that lets the
reader live inside both leads, each with a distinct cadence — propulsive at the chapter level,
lingering at the sentence level when feeling demands it. The effect is escapism with a real
ache underneath.
