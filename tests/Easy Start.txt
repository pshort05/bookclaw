June recently moved from NYC near her small town in New England.  She is a nurse and has accepted a new job at the prestigeous regional hospital in Northern CT. She is settling in and meets a handsome and charming man, Charles, while getting coffee on Saturday.  They talk until after lunch and really hit it off.  They make a date for later that week.  The conversation never mentions what they do.  When she starts her next shift, she finds out he is the well known Dr running another highly respected division of the hospital.  His patients love him, but the staff is afraid of him as he runs a very tight ship.  He also has a reputation of being a ladies man (but is actually not and does nothing to change it)
